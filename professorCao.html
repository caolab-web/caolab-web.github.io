曹　建庭 (Cao Jianting)

1983年上海工業大学電気工学科卒。同年中国地質鉱産省海洋地質調査局技術装備研究所に入所。1993年千葉大学大学院研究科修士課程修了。1996年同大学院自然科学研究科博士課程修了。博士（工学）。同年理化学研究所フロンティア研究に入所，研究員。1997年同研究所脳科学総合研究センター発足に伴い，移籍，研究員。1998年同研究所の脳科学総合研究センター又は革新知能統合センター客員研究科学者兼任（現在に至る）。1998年上智大学電気電子工学科，助手。2002年同大学講師。同年，埼玉工業大学電子工学科准教授。2008年同大学情報システム学科教授，現在に至る。信号処理全般，脳信号処理，神経回路，学習アルゴリズム等の研究に従事。1996年電気通信普及財団による最優秀論文賞受賞，2005年IEEE Circuit and System Societyによる最優秀論文賞受賞，2007年信号処理研究会による最優秀論文賞受賞，2010年International Neural Network Societyによる最優秀論文賞受賞，2019年Asia Pacific Society for Computer and Information Technology SocietyによるOutstanding Research Achievement AWARD受賞，同年IEEE Signal Processing Societyによる最優秀論文賞受賞。IEEE，The New York Academy of Science，電子情報通信学会，信号処理研究会各会員。

Jianting Cao received the M. Eng. and Ph.D. degrees from the Graduate School of Science and Technology, Chiba University, Japan, in 1993 and 1996, respectively. From 1983 to 1988, he worked as a Researcher at the Institute of Technology and Equipment in the Ministry of Geological and Mineral in China. From 1996 to 1998, he worked as a Researcher at the Brain Science Institute, RIKEN (The Institute of Physical and Chemical Research) in Japan. From 1998 to 2002, he worked as an Assistant, and a Lecture at the Sophia University in Japan. From 2002 to 2007, he worked as an Associate Professor at the Saitama Institute of Technology in Japan. He is currently working as a Professor at the Department of Information Systems, Saitama Institute of Technology, and a Visiting Research Scientist at the Advanced Intelligence Project (AIP), RIKEN in Japan. He received the Best Paper Award from the Telecommunications Advancement Foundation (Japan) in 1996, from the IEEE Circuits and System Society in 2005, from the Signal Processing Institute (Japan) in 2007, from International Neural Network Society in 2010, and from IEEE Signal Processing Society in 2010. In the same year he received the Outstanding Research Achievement AWARD from Asia Pacific Society for Computer and Information Technology Society. His research interests include signal processing, biomedical signal processing, neural networks and learning algorithms. Dr. Cao is a member of IEEE, and IEICE (Japan).
